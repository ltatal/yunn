Twinkle Twinkle

Pruduct Concept

Domestication is " to build relationship", if you tame me, then we shall need each other. To me , you will be unique in all the world. To you, I shall be unique in all the world. In the universe, there are two beautiful worlds. The weather is cold and the ground is frozen. The other is a light such as fireflies, fish swim leisurely in which they are residents of this world. By transforming a fish tank into a planet, Twinkle Twinkle let people re-experience the relationship between fish and human at home. As protagonist stringing this world, the fishes are residents, and also the objects being domesticated. In Little Prince worldview as a concept, the tank turned into planets. "Domestication is to build relationships," "if you tame me, then we shall need each other " as a starting point for this point of view, people can re-experience the relationship between fish and human at home. Two works made with double glazing, one is to do with the weather bottle, it combined with the external temperature of crystallization will produce snow imagery; another material to make use of the firefly flashes, feeling special world of fantasy.
